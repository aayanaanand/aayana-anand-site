About Me

Hey! I'm Aayana, and I'm a current freshman at the University of Michigan Ann Arbor. I'm double majoring in Informatics and Creative Writing. I was born in Sarnia, Ontario, Canada, but I've spent most of my life in Michigan.

I've always been a problem-solver at heart, frequently occupying my days with puzzles of all types. Naturally, when I discovered code, I loved that I could approach projects like puzzles, with each line of code being a piece of a bigger picture or desired solution. Using coding as a springboard, I've applied my skills in various languages to explore subsets of technology such as data analysis, software engineering, and consulting. My current interests are in biomedicine, bioinformatics, and consulting, but I'm open and excited to play in whichever interdisciplinary field embraces me next. Wherever in this big umbrella of tech I ultimately find my footing in, I'm dedicated to using technology and collaborative problem-solving skills to solve our world's greatest problems.

My favorite show is The Office U.S., my favorite singer is Taylor Swift, and my sun sign is Leo. Some of my favorite things to do in my freetime are hanging out with friends, writing, building suburban homes in The Sims 4, updating my Taylor Swift song rankings, and trying out new coffee shops on campus.
